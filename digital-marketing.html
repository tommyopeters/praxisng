Crowd follows the spotlight. If you aren't under the spotlight, you'll definitely miss the cherry on the cake. Same rule is applicable in web world. Your website supposed to be easily reachable to your targeted audience when they are looking for the products and services that you sell. Digital Marketing is the key tool that can help you best to improve visibility of your website and makes you easily accessible to your potentials customers.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

While every website owner blames the look and feel of their website for poor RIO and argues with their vendor, the bottom line is that most of your targeted visitors can't reach you. People in general use search engine to find out your website and that's the prime reason to hire professional SEO service provider.

SEO is the most natural and proven way to improve ranking of keywords/key-phrases in Search Engine Result Page (SERP) for a webpage. It works slow but steadily. At Praxis Technologies we provide strategy driven professional SEO service that includes both On-Page and Off-Page optimization.

What We Do?

  • In-depth analysis to find out opportunities and obstacles clearly
  • Developing short-term and/or long-term strategy as per analysis
  • Close monitoring of result and if necessary changing strategy

This is where a professional social media marketing service becomes significant.

Pay-Per-Click (PPC)

When the house is on fire, digging a well isn't of any use. SEO and SMM is a slow process; though, there shouldn't be any argument on its effect. But, when you need an immediate boost in sale, there isn't any better and efficient choice than Pay Per Click Service in Nigeria. And the most worthy point is that you'll have full control on your expense. Also you can track and analyze every click to make it more robust and reduce the costing.

Pay Per Click Campaign is actually a paid advertisement campaign organized by different search engines. You can control the per month, week or even day's expense here and you'll be charged only if someone visits your site by clicking these advertisements. Generally it costs very reasonably and you get a clear report of Cost Per Click. It's highly useful to get quick result in case of seasonal sale or just to let people know about a newly launched product, service or even a website.

What We Do?

  • Create New PPC Ad Account
  • Create Ads on Different Keywords
  • Ensuring Reach to Area-wise Target Audience
  • Implement Professional Tricks to Reduce False Clicks
  • Reduce Per Click Cost through Proven Methods
  • Improve CTR & Quality Score to Ensure Quicker ROI

Online Reputation Management (ORM)

Even if you have millions of happy customer, everyone highlights the few unhappy ones. It leaves very bad impact on any business and you may lose several potential customers, if not the loyal ones. It's not legally or ethically possible to remove those negative comments or news. In such scenario, Online Reputation Management is the perfect service that can help you regain your damaged reputation and burry those bad ones deep under the web-layers.

Actually Online Reputation Management Service is a derivative of Search Engine Optimization service and becomes very handy when you don't want to invest in SEO but need to revive your lost reputation and above all the trust on the brand or person. It may take a few weeks to several months depending upon several facts; however the important part is it's never a bad deal for any business.

What We Do?

  • Sentiment Analyzing of Posts
  • Respond to Unhappy Customers
  • Post Proactive Contents to Overlap the Negatives
  • Find Influences if Any & Take Intelligent Steps
